ABOUT

I'm a 21 years old web development student at Centre d'Estudis Politècnics in Barcelona, Spain; where I previously graduated as a multiplatform app developer.

I've always had an interest in mobile app development, specially in Android. That was the spark that led me to start a 2 years long studies and get a High National Diploma in Multiplatform App Development.
That wasn't it, I wanted to know more and knew the advantages of also covering the web development aspect, hence starting an extra year heavily focused on frontend and backend development.

Since the very start of my programming career, I've procured to create well organised programs and spend additional time on attention to quality.
I would describe myself as someone who likes getting things done, I always share my viewpoint about any problem or solution and I'm open to hear others opinion. If I'm working in a team, I like being heard, so do I to others. I prefer a team where everybody acts as a leader so each one is pushed to improve themselves, if not, I don't doubt about taking leadership.
Five of the projects in my portfolio have been developed alongside 1, 2 or 3 colleagues, I know the importance of good organization and communication between team members and that's why we have always delivered a solid product.

For a considerate large portion of my lifetime, I have played basketball with my school team, and from time to time some of us still gather to play pick-up basketball which brings back good memories. I consider myself a casual videogame player, I've enjoyed playing videogames since I was 5 years old. My entertainment sources mainly come from streams on twitch and videos from youtube, watching high quality content, preferably about tech. I've had other transitory hobbies during my life, as a teenager, sneakers for example. These past 2 or 3 years I've been interested in mechanical keyboards.

As for my future plans, currently having a great foundation regarding frontend and backend, I will dive deep down in backend and improve the robustness of my next coming projects.
